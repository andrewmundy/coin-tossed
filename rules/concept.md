## Mechanics

- Flip the coin
- Heads the coin gains souls
- Tails you lose some of your own soul to the coin
- 3 tails in a row and you die
- Gain souls to charge gems
- Charged gems can be slotted into your coin
- Upgrade your coin for more slots and more souls
- Each playthrough is a completely different character

## Bosses

- Boss phase you run into enemies and you must suck out their souls
- Bosses are actual Egyptian leaders, philosophers, and inventors who used this power for great strength, wisdom, power
  - [Plotinus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plotinus)
  - [Ptahhotep](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ptahhotep)
  - [Pharoh Teos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Teos_of_Egypt)

## Meta progression

- You die but some souls are still trapped in the coin
- The next game starts with some power or gems already slotted
- You play again, but as a new character, your old character lays in the cave dead
